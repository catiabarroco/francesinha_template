Francesinha

Francesinha is a sandwich from the city of Porto, in Portugal.

The francesinha in its special francesinha sandwich variation is more commonly made up of sausage, fresh sausage, ham, cold meats and beef steak, topped with later melted cheese. It is garnished with a tomato, beer and piri-piri-based sauce and can be served with French fries as an accompaniment. The addition of a fried egg on top of the sandwich is an increasingly recurrent fact, and this practice constitutes a change to the original recipe. In some houses, the so-called normal francesinha is served without steak.
